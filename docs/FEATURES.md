# EcoSphere — Complete Feature List

> This document combines: 1. **Mandatory features** from the Odoo EcoSphere problem statement. 2. **High-impact differentiators** that can make the project stand out.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Mandatory Features (Must Build)

## Environmental Module

- Configure Emission Factors
- Product ESG Profiles
- Automatic Carbon Emission Calculation
- Carbon Transactions
- Department Carbon Tracking
- Sustainability Goals
- Environmental Dashboard
- Environmental Reports

## Social Module

- CSR Activities
- Employee Participation
- Diversity Metrics
- Training Completion Tracking
- Evidence Upload
- Approval Workflow
- Social Dashboard
- Social Reports

## Governance Module

- ESG Policies
- Policy Acknowledgements
- Audits
- Compliance Issues
- Issue Ownership
- Due Dates
- Overdue Detection
- Governance Dashboard
- Governance Reports

## Gamification Module

- Sustainability Challenges
- Challenge Lifecycle (Draft → Active → Under Review → Completed → Archived)
- XP System
- Badges
- Rewards
- Leaderboards
- Reward Redemption
- Badge Auto Award

## Dashboard

- Overall ESG Score
- Environmental Score
- Social Score
- Governance Score
- Department Scores
- Organization Dashboard

## Reports

- Environmental / Social / Governance / ESG Summary Reports
- Custom Report Builder
- Export PDF, Excel, CSV
- Filters: Department, Employee, Module, Date, Challenge, ESG Category

## Settings & Admin

- Departments, Categories, ESG Policies, ESG Configuration
- Notification Settings, User Roles, Permissions

## Business Rules

- Auto Emission Calculation
- Evidence Required Toggle
- Badge Auto Award Toggle
- Notification System
- Reward Redemption
- Compliance Ownership & Overdue Alerts

# 2. Features That Can Make Us Win

## AI ESG Copilot

- Natural language ESG assistant
- Explain score changes, report summarization, root cause analysis

## Smart Recommendations

- Carbon reduction, CSR, compliance, vendor, and goal optimization suggestions

## ESG Digital Twin

- Live department health, real-time ESG state, drill-down analytics

## Sustainability Simulator

- What-if analysis, score prediction, remote work / fleet / vendor scenarios

## Executive Intelligence

- Daily AI Brief, weekly digest, one-click board report

## Predictive Analytics

- Carbon forecasting, goal completion, compliance risk, engagement prediction

## Smart Dashboards

- Mission Control, live KPI widgets, department comparison, carbon heatmap, ESG timeline

## Advanced Gamification

- Department Leagues, Weekly Missions, Team Challenges, ESG Streaks
- Secret Achievements, Monthly Champion, Seasonal Events

## AI-powered Evidence Verification

- Image quality validation, duplicate detection, AI proof descriptions

## Smart Notifications

- Priority alerts, AI action suggestions, risk and goal reminders

## Advanced Reports

- Narrative ESG, BRSR/GRI-style, interactive dashboards

## Visual Analytics

- Sankey diagrams, radar charts, heatmaps, carbon breakdown, department comparison

## Productivity

- Quick Actions, Global Search, Keyboard Shortcuts, Saved Views, Favorites, Recent Activities

## Mobile Experience

- Fully responsive, PWA, mobile approvals, mobile dashboards
